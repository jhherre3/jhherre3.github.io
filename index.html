What I do

Embedded hardware design, electrical automation, and R&D problem-solving—taking ideas from concept to working prototype through rigorous bring-up, testing, and iteration.

How I work

Fast prototypes with strong fundamentals: power integrity, safe states, measurable validation, and clear documentation. The goal is reliable systems, not just demos.

Where I’m headed

Robotics and UAS systems (any domain) while continuing to deepen embedded hardware and PCB-driven product development. Long-term: build this into a business.
